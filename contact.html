Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with our services?

Contact us via phone or email to schedule an initial consultation. We'll discuss your needs, explain our services, and work with your case manager to develop a personalized care plan.

Are your staff qualified and background checked?

Yes, all our staff undergo comprehensive background checks, receive ongoing training, and are fully qualified to provide HCBS services according to Minnesota DHS standards.

Can family members be involved in the care plan?

Absolutely! We encourage family involvement and offer services specifically designed to include family training and support.

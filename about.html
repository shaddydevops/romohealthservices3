Our Story

About ROMI Health Services

A trusted partner in home and community-based care, dedicated to enhancing the lives of individuals with disabilities across Minnesota.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, person-centered care services that empower individuals with disabilities to live with dignity, independence, and joy within their communities. We are committed to delivering compassionate, professional support that respects each person's unique goals and aspirations.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of home and community-based services in Minnesota, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and person-centered care. We envision a future where every individual we serve achieves their fullest potential.

Our Journey

Building a Legacy of Care

ROMI Health Services was founded with a simple but powerful belief: everyone deserves to live with dignity, independence, and support tailored to their unique needs. Located in Newport, Minnesota, we've built our reputation on providing exceptional home and community-based services (HCBS) to individuals with disabilities.

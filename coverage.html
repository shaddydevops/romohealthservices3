Coverage Area

Seven Counties, One Commitment

Expanding the Reach of Care

ROMI Health Services proudly serves individuals with disabilities across seven counties in Minnesota. Our strategic location in Newport allows us to efficiently provide services throughout the greater Twin Cities area, ensuring timely, reliable, and quality care to communities across the region.

Whether you're in the heart of the metro or in surrounding communities, our team is ready to bring exceptional, person-centered support directly to you.

Washington County

Providing comprehensive HCBS waiver services to the eastern metro, including Stillwater, Woodbury, and our home base of Newport.

Ramsey County

Full service coverage for St. Paul and surrounding suburbs, focusing on community integration and independent living.

Anoka County

Extending our reach to northern communities to ensure high-quality care is accessible to all residents in Anoka.

Dakota County

Serving the southern metro area with specialized support plans tailored to the unique needs of the Dakota County community.

Carver County

Reliable, person-centered health services for the southwestern communities of Carver County.

Scott County

Dedicated support teams available for residents in Shakopee, Savage, and the greater Scott County region.

Hennepin County

Extensive coverage across Minneapolis and the surrounding suburbs, providing top-tier HCBS waiver programs.

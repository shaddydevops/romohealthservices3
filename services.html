What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive, person-centered care services designed to enhance independence, dignity, and quality of life for individuals with disabilities across Minnesota.

01

Adult Companion Services

Our Adult Companion Services provide meaningful social engagement and companionship to enhance daily living and combat isolation. We understand that social connection is vital to overall wellbeing, and our compassionate companions are trained to provide engaging and supportive presence.

  • ✓ One-on-one companionship and social interaction
  • ✓ Assistance with recreational activities and hobbies
  • ✓ Community integration support
  • ✓ Emotional support and active listening
  • ✓ Help maintaining social connections
👥
02

Individual Community Living Supports (ICLS)

Personalized support designed to help individuals thrive independently within their communities. ICLS services empower clients to participate fully in community life while developing skills for greater independence.

  • ✓ Community integration and participation
  • ✓ Development of independent living skills
  • ✓ Support for employment and education goals
  • ✓ Transportation assistance
  • ✓ Social and recreational activity support
🏘️
03

Night Supervision

Overnight monitoring and assistance to ensure safety and comfort throughout the night. Our night supervision services provide peace of mind for families while ensuring clients receive attentive care during nighttime hours.

  • ✓ Overnight presence and monitoring
  • ✓ Assistance with nighttime routines
  • ✓ Emergency response during night hours
  • ✓ Sleep pattern monitoring
  • ✓ Morning preparation support
🌙
04

Respite Care Services - In Home

Temporary relief care for family caregivers in the comfort of your own home. Our in-home respite care provides families with essential breaks while ensuring loved ones receive quality care in familiar surroundings.

  • ✓ Flexible scheduling (hourly to multi-day)
  • ✓ Care in familiar home environment
  • ✓ Continuation of regular routines
  • ✓ Qualified, background-checked caregivers
  • ✓ Emergency respite availability
🏠
05

Respite Care Services - Out of Home

Alternative respite care options in safe, supportive environments outside the home. Our out-of-home respite services offer variety and new experiences while giving family caregivers needed breaks.

  • ✓ Safe, approved care environments
  • ✓ Engaging activities and social opportunities
  • ✓ Qualified supervision and support
  • ✓ Flexible duration options
  • ✓ Individual care plans maintained
🌳
06

Individualized Home Support with Training

Customized in-home assistance focused on skill development and increasing independence. This service combines direct support with active training to help individuals develop new capabilities.

  • ✓ Personalized skill development programs
  • ✓ Activities of daily living training
  • ✓ Goal-oriented support plans
  • ✓ Progress tracking and documentation
  • ✓ Adaptive techniques and strategies
📚
07

Individualized Home Support with Family Training

In-home support that includes comprehensive training for family members and caregivers. This service empowers families with knowledge and skills to provide better care and support.

  • ✓ Family caregiver education and training
  • ✓ Hands-on demonstration of care techniques
  • ✓ Resource guidance and support
  • ✓ Communication strategies
  • ✓ Ongoing consultation and support
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
08

Individualized Home Support without Training

Direct in-home support services focused on assistance with daily activities and maintaining quality of life. This service provides hands-on help without the training component.

  • ✓ Personal care assistance
  • ✓ Household task support
  • ✓ Meal preparation and nutrition support
  • ✓ Medication reminders
  • ✓ General daily living assistance
🤲
